Description

A dual wick candle with scent of fresh air, pine needles and fallen leaves combine for a woodsy, earthy scent that evokes warmth and coziness

Directions

Ensure the candle is kept away from any flammable materials, draughts or any sources of heat (e.g. fires, TV's, radiators). keep the wax free from any debris e.g. used matches/wick trimmings.
